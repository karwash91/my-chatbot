DevOpsy Service Restart Guide

To restart a service in DevOpsy:

1. Log in to the DevOpsy CLI.
2. Run: devopsy service list
3. Identify the service ID you want to restart.
4. Run: devopsy service restart <service_id>
5. Confirm status with: devopsy service status <service_id>

Notes:
- A restart will cause ~30 seconds of downtime.
- Use --force if the service is stuck.
